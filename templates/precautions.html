Heart Disease Precautions

Good Condition
  • Eat a balanced diet of fruits and lean proteins
  • Exercise daily (30 mins walk)
  • Reduce sugar & salt intake
  • No smoking, limit alcohol
  • Routine yearly checkups
Medium Condition
  • Start light physical activity
  • Avoid saturated & processed foods
  • Monitor BP & sugar levels
  • Consult doctor every 6 months
  • Practice stress relief like yoga
Bad Condition
  • See cardiologist urgently
  • Strict medication routine
  • Get tests (ECG, ECHO)
  • No fried, salty, or sugary foods
  • Minimal physical activity
  • Keep emergency contacts ready
